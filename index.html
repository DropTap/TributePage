Here's a time line of Adolf Hitler's life:

  • 1889 - Born in Small Village of Austria.
  • 1905 - Adolf hitler moves to vienna, where he acquires his anti-semantic beliefs.
  • 1914 - Hitler, by all accounts was an unusual soldier with a sloppy manner and unmilitary bearing.
  • 1919 - Corporal Adolf Hitler was ordered in September 1919 to investigate a small group in Munich known as the German Workers' Party.
  • 1920 - Adolf Hitler never held a regular job and aside from his time in World War One, led a lazy lifestyle, from his brooding teenage days.
  • 1921 - Hitler was now gaining notoriety outside of the Nazi Party for his rowdy, at times hysterical tirades against the Treaty of Versailles, rival politicians and political groups, especially Marxists, and always the Jews.
  • 1930 - Adolf Hitler, Nazis come to power through the Great Depression.
  • 1933 - His first speech as Chancellor took place on February 10. The Nazis' seizure of power subsequently became known as the Machtergreifung. Hitler established the Reichssicherheitsdienst as his personal bodyguards​.
  • 1940 - Adolf Hitler gave his approval to the Manstein Plan on 17th February, 1940, but it was not activated until the 10th May, when the Luftwaffe bombed Dutch and Belgian airfields and the German Army.
  • 1941 - Operation Barbarossa Begins.
  • 1942 - The Battle of Stalingrad was a battle of World War II between Nazi Germany and its allies and the Soviet Union for control of the city of Stalingrad in southwestern Russia.
  • 1943 - Construction of Treblinka Nazi Extermination Camp is Completed.
  • 1944 - Nazis Begin Evacuation of Kaiserwald Concentration Camp Preceding Arrival of Soviet Forces.
  • 1944 - Partisan Units Liberate Jasenovac Concentration Camp from the Ustaše.
  • 1945 - U.S. 89th Infantry Division Liberates Ohrdruf, the First Sub-Camp of Buchenwald Concentration Camp Complex.
  • 1946 - Adolf Hitler Commits Suicide.
